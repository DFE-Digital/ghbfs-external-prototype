Audio visual solutions

Based on your answers, we think you should use the Kent County Supplies (KCS) framework.

What it offers

This framework covers the provision of a full range of audio visual (AV) and interactive solutions, associated services, support and maintenance, including:

  • Interactive products and solutions
  • Soundfield systems
  • Digital signage
  • Installation and integration
  • Maintenance
  • Projectors
  • Replacement parts
  • Room control systems
  • Servicing
  • Training
  • Visual products (including televisions)

Benefits

  • Competitive pricing and special conditions exclusive to education
  • Orders can be placed via direct award and further competition
  • Easy to use with free and full support on using the framework from KCS Professional Services team

All frameworks also offer:

  • compliance with The Official Journal of the European Union (OJEU) principles and regulations
  • a list of checked suppliers
  • pre-agreed terms and conditions
