Electricity (for supply during 2020-2024)

Based on your answers, we think you should use the Eastern Shires Purchasing Organisation (ESPO) framework.

What it offers

A quick, simple and competitive route to the supply of electricity, where ESPO act as the intermediary to buy electricity in the wholesale market on behalf of customers.

Benefits

  • a dedicated supplier account manager to support customers
  • a team of energy experts to assist customers with queries dedicated to providing advice and resolving customer queries
  • the option of a bill validation service for customers, automated meter reading (AMR) installation and assistance with the installation of new supplies

All frameworks also offer:

  • compliance with the relevant procurement regulations
  • suppliers pre-checked by the approved framework provider
  • pre-agreed terms and conditions
