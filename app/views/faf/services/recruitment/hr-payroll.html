Outsourced human resources including payroll services

Based on your answers, we think you should use the Crescent Purchasing Consortium (CPC) framework.

What it offers

  • human resource (HR) services including specialist recruitment or e-recruitment, human resource consultancy (employee relations), managed services, human resource transactional services (employee resourcing), training, business and administration support services, transformational services, leadership or management, support services and employee support services
  • comprehensive suite of payroll services which are available as a bureau, part-managed or managed service
  • human resource and payroll as an integrated service

The services available are not limited to those listed. Suppliers can make their wider portfolio of human resource and payroll services available to you.

Benefits

  • easy to use
  • offers a complete package for schools wanting to outsource HR and/or payroll

All frameworks also offer:

  • compliance with The Official Journal of the European Union (OJEU) principles and regulations
  • a list of checked suppliers
  • pre-agreed terms and conditions
