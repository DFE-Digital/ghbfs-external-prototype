Our specialists and what they can offer

Our team of procurement specialists can save you time and money by offering free support when you're buying any goods or services for your school.

They can offer advice and guidance to make sure you're buying compliantly at any point, even if your procurement is already in progress.

Our specialists can also review your documents to make sure they are complete and compliant. This includes advice on specification documents, tender documents and evaluation criteria.

For complex or high value procurements, our specialists can help to run it from beginning to end. Complex or high value procurements include one or more of the following:

  • the value is above the public contracts regulations (PCR) threshold of £138,760 plus VAT
  • you're buying for multiple schools
  • the procurement requires the transfer of personnel contracts under the Transfer of Undertakings (Protection of Employment) regulations (TUPE)
  • it's challenging to find suppliers
  • there are problems with supply or prices change regularly, for example energy costs
  • contracts are long term or multi-year

We may also offer you extra help if you're new or not experienced in running these types of procurements. Your procurement must be over the PCR threshold to apply.

Working with us to run a complex or high value procurement

We'll need you to work closely with us to make sure that your procurement is a success. This means that you'll need to:

  • gather information, for example, to create a specification
  • tasks, such as checking documents for accuracy
  • make decisions
  • be available for meetings

We'll advise you on what to gather or what you need to do at each stage of your procurement to be compliant. See what the stages of buying are.

How long the process takes

The time a procurement process takes varies depending on what you're buying.

It typically takes 6 months to run a procurement but can be longer if tasks are not done on time, or if the procurement is complex or high value. For example, if you're doing a procurement for multiple schools or if it requires a transfer of staff contracts. These types of complex procurements can take 12 months or longer.

When we talk to you, we'll advise you on how long your procurement can take.

Depending on the process that's right for your school, we may give you a timeline to follow.

Request support

Make a request for help or support with your procurement by using the Get help buying for schools service.

Request help and support

We'll usually stay in contact by email. Sometimes we may arrange a video call with you, for example if we want to talk with you about something complicated, or we want to discuss the help we can offer.

If you need us to communicate with you in another way, you can let us know when you first make a request for support.

Resources to help you buy

We offer other tools and resources such as:

  • guidance on how to buy and buying routes
  • ways to get support from other schools
  • self-guided tools that you can use for your procurement

Go to the buying for schools guidance to see what we offer.
