Our service

Our team of procurement specialists can help you save time and money by offering free support and guidance when you’re buying any goods or services for your school.

Our specialists can offer you:

  • advice and guidance on buying goods or services at any point, even if your procurement is already in progress to make sure you’re buying compliantly
  • our expertise to help you to review your documents, such as specification documents, tender documents, or evaluation criteria to make sure they are complete and compliant
  • help to run a more complex or high value procurement from beginning to end; examples include one or more of the following types of procurement:

  • high value including those above the public contracts regulations (PCR) threshold (£186,000 plus vat)

  • for multiple schools

  • requires the porting of personnel contracts (TUPE)

  • it's challenging to find suppliers

  • there are problems with supply, or prices change regularly, like energy

  • long term or multi-year contracts

  • where the school is new to, or does not have experience of running a procurement

Once you submit a request for help, we’ll get in touch to discuss the help we can offer you if needed.

Read guidance on buying specific goods and services, such as:

  • the value is above the public contracts regulations (PCR) threshold (£186,000 plus vat)
  • If you’re buying for multiple schools
  • the procurement requires the porting of personnel contracts (TUPE)
  • if it's challenging to find suppliers
  • if there are problems with supply, or prices change regularly for example rising energy costs
  • long term or multi-year contracts
  • if you're new or not experienced in running a procurement

Once you submit a request for help, we’ll get in touch to discuss the help we can offer you if needed.

Working with us to run a complex procurement

We'll need you to work closely with us to make sure that your procurement is a success. This means that you'll need to:

  • gather information, for example, to create a specification
  • complete tasks, such as checking documents for accuracy
  • make decisions
  • be available for meetings

We’ll advise you on what to gather or what you need to do at each step of your procurement to be compliant, and what the timelines will be.See what the stages of buying are.

How long the process takes

The time a procurement process takes varies depending on what you’re buying.

It typically takes 6 months but can be longer if tasks are not done on time, or if the procurement is complex. For example, if you're doing a procurement for multiple schools or if it requires a transfer of staff contracts. These types of complex procurements can take 12 months or longer.

When we talk to you, we'll advise you on how long your procurement can take.

Depending on the process that’s right for your school, we may give you a timeline to follow.

Request support

Make a request for help or support with your procurement by using the Get help buying for schools service.

We'll respond by email within 2 working days once you submit a request.

We'll usually stay in contact by email. Sometimes we may arrange a video call with you, for example if we want to talk with you about something complicated.

If you need us to communicate with you in another way, you can let us know when you first make a request for support.
