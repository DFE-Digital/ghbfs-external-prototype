Print marketplace

Based on your answers, we think you should use the Crown Commercial Service (CCS) framework.

What it offers

A digital system that public sector organisations will be able to access to buy printed material. It will cater to high volume print needs, with a range of cost-effective pricing and across multiple suppliers.

Benefits

  • access to a wide range of pre-approved suppliers - compare prices from trusted suppliers quickly and simply
  • an intuitive online tool that does not need to be installed and no licence needed
  • expert advice and a live chat functionality

All frameworks also offer:

  • compliance with The Official Journal of the European Union (OJEU) principles and regulations
  • a list of checked suppliers
  • pre-agreed terms and conditions
