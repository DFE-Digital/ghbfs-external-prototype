Use this service if you're a state funded school in England.

We can:

  • give you general guidance or specific advice on buying goods or services for your school
  • give you support with buying goods or services for your school using a framework agreement
  • buy on your behalf through a framework agreement
  • buy on your behalf by getting quotes or bids for contract renewals or new procurements from an open supplier market
  • help you buy energy - we'll ask you to upload your recent energy bill to give us the information we need to support you

The Get help buying for schools team will respond to you within 2 working days.
